The Helper Chat Widget allows you to provide real-time support to your customers directly on your website or application. With customizable display options and seamless integration, you can offer a superior support experience tailored to your business needs.

## Key Features

- **Flexible Display Options**: Show the chat widget to all customers or only to high-value customers based on revenue thresholds.
- **Email to Chat Auto-Response**: Automatically redirect email conversations to the chat widget for faster resolution.
- **Secure Authentication**: Protect customer data with HMAC-based authentication.
- **Contextual Help**: Add context-specific help buttons throughout your application.
- **Multiple Integration Options**: Choose from React/Next.js components or vanilla JavaScript implementations.
